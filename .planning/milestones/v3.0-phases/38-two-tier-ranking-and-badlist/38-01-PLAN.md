<objective>
Implement two-tier ranking that sorts LSD solutions by signal match count first (descending), then MAE second (ascending). This prevents the ibuprofen hallucination where wrong cyclohexadiene solutions with MAE 1.93 outranked the correct solution with MAE 2.13 but better signal coverage (13/13 vs 11/13 matches).

Purpose: RANK-01, RANK-02, and RANK-05 requirements. Fixes the core ranking deficiency identified in the Sherlock analysis and adds HOSE radius transparency to ranking output.
Output: Modified ranker with two-tier sort, comprehensive tests, updated CLI output, and radius_used propagation to ShiftAssignment.
</objective>
