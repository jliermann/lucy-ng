Add a new **Confidence Scoring** section to skill/SKILL.md. Place it AFTER the Error Tolerance and Ambiguity Detection section (added by Plan 01) and BEFORE Quick Reference (which is the final section). This section teaches the AI agent to assign and document confidence levels.

The section must contain these subsections:

**Per-Atom Confidence Factors**

Three factors evaluated qualitatively (NOT as a formula):

1. **Digital resolution** (can this peak be distinguished from neighbors?):
   - High: No other carbon within 2x the resolution limit
   - Medium: Another carbon within 2-3x the resolution limit
   - Low: Peaks overlapping or within 1x the resolution limit (detected as ambiguous in Error Tolerance section)

2. **HOSE prediction quality** (MAE for this specific atom from ranking):
   - High: MAE < 2.0 ppm (excellent match between predicted and experimental)
   - Medium: MAE 2.0-3.5 ppm (reasonable match)
   - Low: MAE > 3.5 ppm (poor match or unusual chemical environment)

3. **Number of supporting correlations**:
   - High: 3+ HMBC correlations + HSQC
   - Medium: 1-2 HMBC correlations + HSQC
   - Low: 0 HMBC correlations (quaternary with shift-only constraint) OR HSQC ambiguous

Agent evaluates all three factors and assigns High/Medium/Low based on overall judgment. The >90% / 60-90% / <60% thresholds from requirements are qualitative GUIDELINES for interpretation, NOT computed percentages.

Include a worked example:
```
Carbon 5 (155.08 ppm): MEDIUM confidence
- Resolution: GOOD (nearest carbon 4.3 ppm away, well-resolved)
- HOSE MAE: 2.8 ppm (GOOD, within normal aromatic range)
- Correlations: 1 HMBC (MEDIUM, single quaternary correlation)
-> Overall MEDIUM due to sparse correlations despite good resolution/prediction
```

**Explicit confidence downgrade rules:**
- Any ambiguity detected (from Error Tolerance section) -> at most Medium confidence
- MAE > 3.5 ppm for any atom -> that atom is Low confidence
- 0 HMBC correlations on quaternary carbon -> that atom is Low confidence
- DEPT/HSQC conflict unresolved -> that atom is Medium at best
- Audit question: "Would an expert spectroscopist agree this assignment is >90% certain?"

**Per-Structure Confidence Derivation**

Derive overall structure confidence from atom-level scores. Use threshold-based approach:
- **High**: >= 80% of carbons rated High or Medium, AND no more than 1 Low-confidence carbon
- **Medium**: >= 50% of carbons rated High or Medium, OR 2+ Low-confidence carbons
- **Low**: < 50% of carbons rated High or Medium, OR critical structural atoms (ring junctions, stereogenic centers) are Low

Include statement: "Err on the side of honesty. Better to report Medium confidence and be right than High confidence and be wrong."

**Confidence-Annotated Output Format**

Show the expected output format for the confidence summary:

```markdown
## Assignment Confidence

**Overall structure confidence: MEDIUM**
(8/10 carbons High/Medium, 2 Low-confidence quaternary carbons)

| Carbon | Shift (ppm) | Type | Resolution | HOSE MAE | Correlations | Confidence |
|--------|-------------|------|------------|----------|-------------|------------|
| C1 | 155.08 | Quat | Good | 2.8 | 1 HMBC | Medium |
| C2 | 138.51 | CH | Excellent | 1.2 | 3 HMBC + HSQC | High |
| C3 | 28.5 | CH3* | Good | 1.8 | 2 HMBC + HSQC | Medium |
...
*Multiplicity conflict: DEPT/HSQC disagreement, assigned CH3 based on shift (see Ambiguities)
```

**Suggesting Additional NMR Experiments (CONF-03)**

When confidence is Medium or Low for specific atoms, suggest SPECIFIC experiments that would resolve the uncertainty. These must be actionable for a spectroscopist:

Template examples:
- "Acquire **DEPT-90** to resolve CH/CH3 ambiguity at [shift] ppm (currently assigned as [X] based on shift, but pattern-based inference uncertain)"
- "Acquire **HMBC with optimized nJCH delay** (5 Hz instead of 8 Hz) to enhance quaternary carbon correlations, specifically targeting C=O at [shift] ppm which shows [0/1] correlations"
- "Acquire **higher-resolution HSQC (F1 dimension)** with [2x] F1 points to distinguish [shift_A]/[shift_B] ppm pair (current resolution [X] pts/ppm insufficient)"
- "Re-acquire **DEPT-135** with longer acquisition time to improve S/N for peak at [shift] ppm (current S/N = [X], insufficient for confident multiplicity assignment)"

Each suggestion must explain WHAT experiment, WHY it helps, and WHICH specific atom/issue it resolves.

Show this as a "Recommended Additional Experiments" section in the analysis output format.
