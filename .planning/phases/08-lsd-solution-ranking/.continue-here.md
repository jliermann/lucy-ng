<context>
Key insight from Ibuprofen demonstration: The correct structure may rank lower when experimental
spectrum shows fewer signals due to symmetry (10 signals vs 13 carbons). The predictor outputs
all 13 individual carbon shifts, causing unmatched predictions that incur penalties.

This is a known limitation documented in symmetry-notes.md. Ranking should be used as a guide
to narrow candidates, not as ground truth. Users should review top N candidates.

The HOSE-based predictor works well (MAE ~1.8 ppm for good matches) but has limitations with
ipso carbons in substituted aromatics (predicted ~132 ppm vs experimental ~140 ppm).
</context>
