**Edit 1: Add Check 6 to Section 4 (Chemical Plausibility Assessment)**

Insert AFTER the `**Check 5 -- Natural product likelihood:**` paragraph and BEFORE the `**Report format:**` line. Add:

```
**Check 6 -- Aromatic ring verification:** Parse `warnings` from `lucy lsd rank --format json` output. If the `warnings` array contains an aromatic mismatch warning (text contains "Aromatic ring expected"), flag ALL solutions where `has_aromatic_ring` is `false` as QUESTIONABLE. This means the experimental spectrum shows >= 4 shifts in 110-160 ppm (classic aromatic/alkene sp2 pattern) but LSD produced only non-aromatic structures. Root cause is typically 4J HMBC couplings through aromatic rings — they satisfy the 2-3 bond requirement numerically but exclude the correct aromatic structure. Remediation to report: identify HMBC correlations between aromatic ring carbon positions (110-160 ppm) and benzylic or alpha-substituent positions, remove the most suspect ones (likely 4J), and re-run LSD. Note: use QUESTIONABLE (not IMPLAUSIBLE) because the issue is with LSD constraints, not necessarily with individual structures per se. IMPLAUSIBLE requires multiple independent failures.
```
